About BKR Logistics

Founded in 2021 by Fanuzile Makhubela, BKR Logistics & Transport started with one truck to serve the Nkomazi region. We’ve delivered countless loads of sand and soil, supporting builders and homeowners with reliable, affordable service.

Mission: To deliver high-quality materials with efficiency and care, empowering Nkomazi’s construction projects.
